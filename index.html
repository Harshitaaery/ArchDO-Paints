MBTI Personality Types

The MBTI (Myers-Briggs Type Indicator) classifies people into 16 personality types based on four key traits: Introversion/Extraversion, Sensing/Intuition, Thinking/Feeling, and Judging/Perceiving. It helps individuals understand how they perceive the world and make decisions. Each type reveals strengths, preferences, and how people interact with others.
